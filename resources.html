Vim

I use Vim with a few common plugins, switching between a desktop Linux computer, my MacBook Pro, a couple of VirtualBox environments and a Raspberry Pi. I appreciate the consistency of the experience from platform to platform.

  • My dotfiles
  • The Gruvbox color theme
  • Pathogen
  • Nerdtree
  • Vim-airline
  • Vim-autopep8
  • Vim-fugitive

I used to spend a lot of time in Atom, but as I jump from computer to computer I found the Vim experience more consistent across machines.
